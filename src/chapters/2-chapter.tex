\subsubsection{Variation of properties between machined and other components}

Unlike machined components that are extracted from a bulk of material, other 
components manufactured by alternative processes may exhibit higher levels of
property variations along its volume. 

\subsubsection{Examples of components}
Unlike machined components that are extracted from a bulk of material that underwent
similar cooling conditions, components manufactured by directed energy deposition
may exhibit higher levels of property variations along its volume.

\subsubsection{Relevance of testing miniaturized samples}
Testing miniaturized samples, with dimensions ranging from 0.5 mm to 10 mm, becomes
a specially relevant topic when we are interested in characterizing critical
regions of small components, or assessing the variation of properties along its 
volume.



In these cases, it is not enough to have information on the mechanical properties
measured from samples extracted from bulk materials.
